About Lunch Hub


Tradition since 2022

Lunch Hub was founded in 2022 by three Web Engeneering students at the Dalton School. We are a comprehensive software program that is designed to make the lunch experience at Dalton more streamlined and enjoyable

We allow students, faculty, and staff to view crucial and time-saving information about the length of the lunch line, foods of the day, and future lunch options. We provide the never seen before oppurtunity for Dalton community members to become more involved in the Lunch decision making
